Why it matters

Most people overestimate how much they study.

Research consistently shows people believe they work 2–3× more than they actually do. Passive distractions — a quick video, a few minutes of scrolling — compound invisibly. Without data, there's no way to improve.

How it works

Simple rules, running quietly.

01

You define your study spaces study

Add your work sites below — Google Docs, Notion, your LMS. Time on these pages counts as focused work.

02

The soft timer watches your tabs

Leave a study page and a grace period starts. Stay away and a distraction timer begins. Switch back and it stops immediately.

03

Every distraction is logged distraction

YouTube, Reddit, social media — each off-task visit is recorded with its URL and exact duration.

04

Idle time closes segments automatically

No activity for your set idle threshold? The current segment closes. Screen locks and minimized windows work the same way.

05

Click "Done Studying" for your report

The timer popup generates a full breakdown — total time, focus ratio, top distracting sites, and actionable recommendations.
